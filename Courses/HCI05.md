### 5. Education & Work

Think of how you learn, how you study, and how you work. Reading a book, watch videos, listening to an online lecture through your laptop or mobile phone, doing a team project, talking in KakoTalk for discussion, ... all the activities related to education and work are conducted via any type of INTERACTIONs. Everyone learns knowledge or technique from a certain way of interaction. In the past, knowledge transfer was made by people-to-people interaction. Today, various computer technologies support people to learn and work so that modern people can treat a lot of information and knowledge quickly in their daily life. But, HCI is a different story. If someone is learning inefficiently, then that could be an HCI-related problem probably. HCI should help people learn efficiently, effectively, and satisfactorily.

Let's see what kind of interactive technologies can support people learn and work efficiently and smartly. Especially, think of 'how to learn on-line & off-line blended situations' under COVID-19 and UNTACT trend, as well as 'how to work'.

Try to keep thinking the detailed (= decomposited) interactions between humans and computers in education & work.

**C. COVID-19 and Chellenges in Education and Work**
