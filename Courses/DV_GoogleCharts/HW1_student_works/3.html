Types of Preferred Assets for Seoulite

▶ date: 2019-09-17

▶ Introduction: This chart shows the types of preferred Assets for Seoulite by Age. It shows that people of all ages in Seoul prefer real estate to financial assets. As the age increases, the ratio of preference for real estate tends to rise.

▶ Data: This is data based on a survey conducted by the Seoul Institute in 2015.
